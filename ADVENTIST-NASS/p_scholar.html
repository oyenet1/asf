Qatar University Scholarships 2020/2021 for International Students

Qatar University offers types of scholarships annually to attract qualified students, provided that they meet the requirements of each scholarship and submet the required documents.

Application Deadline: 5th November 2019

Eligible Countries:International

About the Award: Qatar University supports a wide variety of scholarships recipients each academic year in order to attract highly qualified students. Duration of Award:All scholarship students must commit to maximum duration which is the proposed duration of the academic program that he/she is studying at, which is based on the program graduation requirements, in addition to the number of semesters spent in the Foundation Program (maximum one year). The maximum duration does not include summer courses or courses that the student drops or withdraws.

How to Apply:

  • Applicants must meet the admission requirements for the college and major that they applied to. In addition to, submitting the final, official high school transcript for new admitted applicants.
  • The scholarship must be benefited from in the same semester of the year in which the scholarship was awarded; otherwise, the scholarship will be cancelled in that semester.

Application Condition for international StudentsThe student applied to majors taught in English as a first or second preference, must demonstrate proficiency in English and Mathematics by satisfying the minimum competency requirements by submitting IELTS (5.5) and SAT (570) or ACT (24).
